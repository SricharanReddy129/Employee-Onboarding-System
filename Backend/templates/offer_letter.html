Annexure B: Terms of Employment

1. Background Assessment

This offer letter remains provisional and is granted under the assumption that all documentation, credentials, and references supplied during the interview phase are entirely authentic. Paves Global Infotech will conduct a thorough background verification. Any discovery of fraudulent claims or misrepresentation of prior employment may lead to immediate revocation of this offer or instant termination post-joining.

2. Probation, Evaluation, & Confirmation

Your employment will commence with a standard probationary period spanning 90 days. Throughout this timeframe, your operational performance, integration into the team, and behavioral conduct will be monitored. Following successful completion of the probationary phase, your employment will be formally confirmed via official written communication. Management reserves exclusive rights to increase the duration of this probation if performance benchmarks are not met.

3. Mobility & Deployment

While you will initially be stationed at our corporate headquarters in Hyderabad, the company operates dynamically. As such, we retain complete discretion to transfer, re-allocate, or depute your services to any alternate domestic or international branch, subsidiary, partner organization, or client geographical location aligned with evolving business objectives.

4. Intellectual Property & Non-Disclosure

You agree to adhere unwaveringly to our corporate confidentiality protocols. During your tenure, you will encounter highly sensitive client data, internal infrastructure maps, and proprietary software. It is strictly prohibited to expose internal data to third parties. Furthermore, any software code, architectural design, framework, or intellectual property produced by you during your employment immediately vests with and becomes the exclusive property of the company.

5. Operational Hours

We function on a standard corporate schedule. However, your role demands a commitment to deliverables. You are expected to demonstrate flexibility during critical release cycles, high-priority client deliverables, or global synchronization events that may demand extended or variable operational hours.
